About 108 Journeys

The number 108 is sacred in yoga, meditation, and spirituality, symbolizing wholeness, balance, and the path inward.

At 108 Journeys, we honor this essence by curating retreats that weave together yoga, meditation, culture, and conscious exploration. Every detail destinations, logistics, accommodations, and safety is managed with care, giving teachers the freedom to guide while participants immerse fully in transformation.

Our flagship retreat series, Chapter 1 Awakening in Rishikesh, is rooted in Indias spiritual landscapes. These journeys invite participants into deep stillness and awareness, blending yoga, meditation, and cultural immersion the Capital of Yoga in the world Rishikesh.

"We help yoga teachers and retreat organizers plan an immersive spiritual retreat across India"
