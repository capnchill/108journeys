Who we are

Based out of India (the cradle of Spirituality) , 108 Journeys curates transformative retreats, rooted in yoga, meditation, and conscious exploration. We power yoga and spiritual leaders and help them organise retreats in India. Thereby acting as their on ground partners.

This is for you if

you are a yogic or spiritual leader who wants to get their group or family to India for Spiritual , yogic and ayurvedic retreats.

What do we handle?

We handle every detail from destinations, logistics, safety, and cultural immersion so that teachers can focus on guiding their community. From India to the US, our retreats create space for growth.

Join Us

At 108 Journeys, every retreat is more than travel, its a practice of connection and transformation. We design wellness journeys that blend yoga, meditation, culture, and mindful exploration, balancing structure with freedom. From sunrise in the Himalayas to reflection in ancient temples, nourishing meals, and soulful excursions, each experience nurtures growth and renewal. Whether in India, the US, or beyond, our focus is creating spaces where transformation feels natural, lasting, and deeply meaningful.
